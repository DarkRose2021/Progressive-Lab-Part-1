About Me




Growing Up

I grew up in southern Kentucky with some horses. We didn't really have many neighbors or many cars go by our house. I learned how to ride horses pretty early in my life. About 4 years ago I got my first horse. She was a handful at first, but now she a great horse to let kids learn to ride on. Even though she's a calm horse, once she enters an area, she's a different horse.

Since I grew up kind of in the middle of nowhere, I couldn't really do much at home. Up until 7th grade I went to T.C.Cherry and Bowling Green Middle School. I couldn't have a lot of my friends come over to my house because their parents didn't want their children so far from town. I switched to JEBMS, which was in Allen County, And I never really hung out with people after school becauseI had to take the bus home and my friends didn't really live near me. Making friends and hanging out with them outside of school is hard for me.
